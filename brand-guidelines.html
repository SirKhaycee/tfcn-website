Brand Overview

The Finance Cycle Network (TFCN) is an annual curated gathering of Africa's most serious financial minds: operators, allocators, and visionaries who read cycles before the crowd notices.

The brand is built on clarity, confidence, and substance. No fluff. No motivational theatre. Just the patterns that move money.

Voice & Tone

TFCN speaks to people who are serious about capital. The tone is confident but not arrogant, sharp but not cynical.

Direct, Not Verbose
"The Old Taker reads cycles." Not: "The Old Taker leverages advanced cyclical pattern recognition methodologies."
Confident, Not Motivational
"No fluff. Just patterns." Not: "Unlock your full financial potential and manifest abundance!"
Substantive, Not Buzzword-Heavy
"Africa's most curated finance gathering." Not: "A disruptive ecosystem-driven thought leadership summit."

Design Do's & Don'ts

✓ Do

  • Use bold typography with clear hierarchy
  • Embrace white space and negative space
  • Let orange accents punch through black
  • Use editorial serif italics for depth
  • Keep layouts clean and intentional

✗ Don't

  • Use gradients, neon, or pastel colors
  • Overcrowd layouts with excessive elements
  • Use motivational stock imagery
  • Distort or alter the logo
  • Use generic "conference" design tropes

Photography & Imagery

People: Professional headshots with clean backgrounds. Remove distracting elements. Sharp, confident, well-lit portraits.

Environment: Minimal, architectural, urban. Avoid generic stock photos. If showing venues, focus on structure and space.

Treatment: High contrast, slightly desaturated. Never over-filtered or Instagram-style edits.

Applications

Website: Black/cream backgrounds. Bold sans headlines. Orange CTAs. Generous spacing. Scroll reveals.

Social Media: Gradient backgrounds (black to dark grey). Clean headshots. Orange info bars. White bold headlines. Minimal text.

Print: High contrast black + orange on cream stock. Bold typography. No unnecessary decoration.
